E-Commerce Catalog

UX/UI

But what is it?

The E-Commerce Catalog is a project that aims to connect stores and and the best service providers in an easy and transparent way, with over a thousand companies registered and registering everyday. My job here was to redesign the whole experience of utilizing the catalog, both for the user searching for a provider, and for the companies who have to maintain their profiles in the plataform in a seamless way. After designing various user flows, dashboards and input fields, I am very happy with the result. This is a work in progress and is being developed as you read this.
