iMasters

UX/UI

But what is it?

iMasters is an amazing project that I had the privilege of working on. Since 2001, they've created a thriving community of developers, capacitating them and developing the field at the same time, through courses, webinars, articles and conferences through the country.

My job was, and still is, redesign the whole experience, rethinking user flows and make the website more user-friendly. Working side by side with the development team, we are achieving great results and this is the project that I'm currently working on. There are lots of challenges to deal with when working on a project of this size, with a whole lot of legacy content to take into account. It's been really fun, and you can expect it to launch it soon, so here's a little sneak peek :)
